Blockers & Resolutions

‚úì Resolved

Monolithic Feature Structure

The scale of RBAC and multi-tenancy changes made incremental deployment difficult. Lesson learned: Early partitioning during design and planning will allow future features to ship faster in smaller, testable releases.

‚úì Resolved

Infrastructure Capacity Constraints

Backend and frontend resource limits slowed progress on observability and backend environment segregation. Resolution: Prioritized completion of UI and role-based logic while deferring infra-heavy work to the next release.
