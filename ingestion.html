Ingestion & Tax Navigator

Production-Scale Integration & Performance

The Ingestion & Tax Navigator Pod focused on advancing FlexiGenAI's ingestion, inference, and integration capabilities to power the launch of the Tax Navigator AI platform, EY's first large-scale client deployment. Through deep collaboration, the pod achieved 4-6X Faster ingestion & from 50-80% accuracy, introduced intelligent load balancing and batching, and integrated FlexiGenAI directly into the Tax Navigator UI via secure APIs and embedded AI assistants.

Product Objectives

Enhance ingestion performance and scalability
Improve inference accuracy
Enable end-to-end integration with Tax Navigator
Introduce AI assistants (AIRA & AIDA)
Create sustainable ingestion architecture
Ensure production-grade stability
